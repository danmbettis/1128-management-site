How It Works

Simple, structured onboarding

Designed for clarity, speed, and clean documentation.

1) Intake Request

Submit your intake request with the details needed for Mississippi C-Corp and EIN coordination. You select what you want 1128 to coordinate and manage.

2) Review & Approval

We review your submission, confirm scope, and create your client portal login after approval.

3) Setup & Coordination

We coordinate the setup tasks you authorized and track progress in your portal checklist.

4) Ongoing Management

As authorized, 1128 can continue administrative management and status updates until the business is operationally sustainable, or until authorization is revoked in writing.
