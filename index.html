Field Evaluation & Operations Review

A confidential, hands-on evaluation — similar to an “Undercover Boss” approach. We discreetly observe operations, customer flow, and team interactions to uncover blind spots, policy gaps, and training needs.

Reduce shrink and downtime, improve service consistency, and align staff to expectations. We focus on quick wins and sustainable fixes.
