Our Services

1128 Management Corp provides administrative, organizational, and management services for businesses operating as Mississippi C-Corporations.

  • IRS EIN coordination
  • Business name documentation
  • Business address, phone, and email setup

  • Banking setup coordination
  • Payment processing setup coordination
  • Corporate documentation organization
  • Ongoing administrative business management
  • Operational consulting & internal process review (owner-level oversight)

  1. Submit Intake Request — Provide basic business and ownership details.
  2. Review & Authorization — We confirm scope and obtain authorization.
  3. Setup & Coordination — We coordinate filings, systems, and documentation.
